Choose Your Foreign Payment Weapon

BookMyForex's range of forex cards are designed to meet the needs of every traveller. No matter which country you're travelling to or for what purpose, we've got the perfect solution for your international payment needs.

One size doesn't fit all and the same holds true for your international payment needs. We have crafted our forex cards after studying customer behaviour and listening to your demands. Our application automatically selects the best forex card for you based on your needs and our capabilities

Book My Forex - Yes Bank Co-branded Multi Currency Forex Card

The BookMyForex Yes Bank Forex Card is a multi-currency card on which you can load 14 foreign currencies. It also allows you to convert the foreign currency balance you have back into IN and spend your remaining balance at online and physical stores in India
The multi-currency forex card is the perfect option for most leisure and business travellers, traveling to commonly visited foreign destinations. The card lets you load foreign currency at true zero markup rates without any Visa/ MasterCard markups and make poyments overseas in the most cost effective manner possible

  • Load 14 Currencies At Fixed Exchange Rates
  • Zero Markup Forex Rates
  • Student Favourite - Free Payments to Universities and Banks
  • Zero Cross Currency Charges
  • Zero ATM Withdrawal Fees
  • Delivered in 2 Working Days

The single currency card can be couriered across India and can also be door delivered in 20+ cities where we have a physical presence. In case of door delivery, customers can receive the card on the same working day if the order with full payment and documents is placed before 1 pm. For customers located outside these 20 cities, the card will be delivered in 2 - 3 days post placing the order.

The single currency card is the default choice for international students as it lets students load funds in US Dollars, which does not depreciate like the Indian rupee. Thanks to unlimited free ATM withdrawals students can easily withdraw cash whenever they need. However, the top reason for students to opt for this card is the 0 cash disbursal/ 0 cash @ POS charges. Thanks to this, students can pay their tuition fees or make deposits into their local bank accounts with zero forex markup and with no fee at all. This is a great alternative to using traditional wire transfers or services like Flywire/ Conver and can save students lakhs of rupees in forex charges over the span of their course. FAQs - Everything you need to know

A forex card is a prepaid travel card that allows you to load multiple foreign currencies and use them abroad.

You can use a prepaid travel card at any location abroad that accepts Visa or Mastercard, including stores, restaurants, hotels, ATMs, and e-commerce websites.

A multi-currency forex card can hold multiple foreign currencies such as USD, EUR, GBP, AUD, CAD, and more.

You can apply online through banks or fintech companies offering zero forex markup cards, usually by submitting KYC documents.

It typically takes 1-3 working days to receive a prepaid travel card after applying.
